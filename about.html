ABOUT

Beeer is an all new way to keep track of the beers you've tried, and discover new ones. Our database of user reviewed beers is growing everyday and we'd love you to be a part of it. Whether you're just a beer fan or an avid taster, Beeer lets you keep track of, search and share, every detail of the brews you come across.

At it's core, Beeer is a simply designed and yet uber detailed beer journal. What makes it different, is the community of Beeer users. Every time a beer is recorded it becomes part of the WallofBeeer.com, accessible to anyone. This growing list of reviews allows you to find out all about what other people are drinking and what they thought of it.

Join Beeer today in our quest to try every beer in the world.

EXPLANATION

WallofBeeer.com posts entries from the Beeer app. Beers that have only a single entry are simply posted to the wall. If it has more then one, the information is combined into an aggregate profile, which shows up with an orange border. Information that is opinion based, such as rating and taste, is averaged, while information that based on facts, such as style, is majority ruled.

That's the basic functionality for now. Check back here in the future to see updates on features that we're working on.
